Discovering Rome: The Eternal City

Welcome to Rome, Italy, the Eternal City! As an American tourist, you're about to embark on a journey through a city with an incomparable blend of history, culture, and culinary delights. Let's delve into the marvels that await you in the heart of Italy.

The Colosseum

Your adventure begins with a visit to the Colosseum, an ancient amphitheater where gladiators once battled. Stand in awe of its grandeur and imagine the roars of the crowd.

Roman Forum

Wander through the Roman Forum, the political and cultural heart of ancient Rome. Marvel at the remnants of temples, arches, and government buildings.

Pantheon

Step inside the Pantheon, a perfectly preserved Roman temple. Its majestic dome is a testament to ancient engineering.
